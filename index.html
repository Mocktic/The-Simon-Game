Instructions

  • Welcome to "The Simon Game"
  • This game is based on your short term memory skills.
  • Start the game by pressing any key on your keyboard.
  • You can select a color by clicking on the color with your cursor.
  • To move to the next level you have to follow the same color sequence played by the computer.
  • After each level the speed of changing color will increase
  • If you miss any color in the sequence, you'll lose the game.
  • So, Keep Learning, Keep Moving Forward!
